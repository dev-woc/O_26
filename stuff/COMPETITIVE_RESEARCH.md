# Competitive Research: Similar Projects and Initiatives

## Overview

This research examines existing projects that combine blockchain technology, cryptocurrency, and social impact initiatives, particularly those serving homeless populations and vulnerable communities. The findings help inform Project Beacon's unique positioning and identify opportunities for innovation.

## Digital Identity Projects for Homeless Populations

### Austin's MyPass Initiative (2018-Present)

**Overview**: Austin's MyPass is a blockchain-enabled platform that stores, secures, validates, and automatically packages personal documents for homeless individuals.

**Key Features**:
- Secure digital document storage
- Blockchain-based identity verification
- Medical records integration
- Multi-device access (computer, smartphone, SMS)
- Complete user ownership and control
- GDPR-compliant deletion capabilities

**Target Problem**: 50% of people experiencing homelessness lack proper ID, taking 2-4 weeks to replace documents.

**Development Approach**: 
- Two years of community research
- Ongoing advisory input from homeless individuals
- Hackathon weekend with blockchain community
- Emphasis on "low friction" user experience
- User testing with target population

**Funding**: Robert Wood Johnson Foundation Pioneering Ideas in Technology and Health Grant

**Current Status**: Active pilot with ongoing development

### NYC Fummi App by Blockchain for Change (2017-2018)

**Overview**: Mobile app providing homeless individuals with smartphones and blockchain-based digital identity management.

**Key Features**:
- Free smartphone distribution (3,000 units)
- Digital identity management
- Access to shelters and food pantries
- Financial services integration
- Internal ecosystem to minimize transaction costs
- Permanent, immutable blockchain-based ID storage

**Partners**: Urban Pathways, Part of the Solution (POTS), Hakook, Robin Hood Foundation

**Current Status**: Completed pilot program

## Blockchain-Based Universal Basic Income Projects

### GoodDollar (2021-Present)

**Overview**: Decentralized protocol delivering UBI through blockchain technology with innovative Web3 funding model.

**Key Metrics (2024)**:
- Over 300,000 users in 16 months
- 24+ million transactions on Celo network
- Drives nearly 50% of daily Celo activity
- 100,000+ new users after V4 launch

**Technology Stack**:
- Originally Ethereum, migrated to Celo (2024)
- Gasless transactions
- Sybil-resistant identity (GoodID)
- Mobile-optimized for low-bandwidth regions

**2024 Innovations**:
- **GoodOffers**: Targeted income streams for specific communities
- Conditional income flows without additional KYC
- Support for NGOs, DAOs, and donor-directed funding
- Mobile top-up payments through Fonbnk

**Use Cases**:
- Women's cooperative support in Brazil
- Conservation UBI pilot with Climate Collective
- Mobile payment integration

**Unique Value Proposition**: Direct micro-payments globally without traditional banking infrastructure

### Circles UBI (2021-2023)

**Overview**: Community Currency System allowing users to mint personal tokens at fixed rate (1 token/hour).

**Key Features**:
- Personal cryptocurrency creation for each user
- Continuous token minting through smart contracts
- Trust-network based value exchange
- Community-driven governance

**Location**: Berlin, Germany pilot

**Research Findings**: 
- Deep community value alignment
- Strong identification with project ideals
- Concluded operations in December 2023

**Current Status**: Pilot completed, research data available

## Key Insights and Market Gaps

### What's Working

1. **Digital Identity Solutions**:
   - Strong community engagement and user testing
   - Focus on document storage and verification
   - Multi-device accessibility
   - User ownership and control

2. **UBI Distribution**:
   - Successful mobile-first approaches
   - Gasless transactions reduce barriers
   - Community-driven governance models
   - Integration with existing payment systems

3. **Technology Adoption**:
   - Layer 2 solutions (Celo, Polygon) improving accessibility
   - Growing mobile crypto wallet adoption (29M users June 2024)
   - Smart contract automation reducing operational costs

### Market Gaps and Opportunities

1. **Earning Mechanisms**:
   - **Gap**: Most projects focus on passive UBI or document storage
   - **Opportunity**: Activity-based earning rewards positive behaviors

2. **Comprehensive Service Integration**:
   - **Gap**: Limited integration between identity, payments, and service redemption
   - **Opportunity**: Full-cycle platform from earning to spending

3. **Partner Ecosystem**:
   - **Gap**: Limited local business integration
   - **Opportunity**: Comprehensive partner network with local services

4. **Geographic Focus**:
   - **Gap**: Most pilots are single-city focused
   - **Opportunity**: Scalable multi-city platform design

5. **Incentive Alignment**:
   - **Gap**: Limited connection between aid and community improvement
   - **Opportunity**: Mutual benefit system encouraging community participation

## Differentiation Opportunities for Project Beacon

### 1. Activity-Based Earning Model
Unlike passive UBI distribution, Project Beacon enables users to earn through:
- Community service activities
- Skill development programs
- Job training participation
- Daily engagement rewards

### 2. Comprehensive Local Integration
- Deep partnership with Orlando-specific organizations
- Local business participation in redemption network
- Integration with existing city services and programs

### 3. Mutual Benefit Design
- Community improvement activities benefit everyone
- Partner businesses gain customers and social impact recognition
- Transparent impact measurement and reporting

### 4. Privacy-First Approach
- Minimal PII collection (learning from GDPR requirements)
- User-controlled data sharing
- Anonymous analytics while maintaining security

### 5. Sustainable Economic Model
- Closed-loop economy prevents speculation
- Fixed USD pegging maintains stability
- Clear path to financial sustainability through partner fees

## Lessons Learned

### Critical Success Factors

1. **Community-Centered Design**: Austin's success with extensive user research and ongoing advisory input
2. **Low-Friction Experience**: Essential for users with limited digital literacy or device access
3. **Trust and Transparency**: Blockchain provides accountability but must be communicated clearly
4. **Sustainable Funding**: Need for clear economic model beyond grant funding
5. **Local Partnership**: Success requires deep integration with existing service providers

### Common Challenges

1. **Technology Barriers**: Need for simplified interfaces and offline capabilities
2. **Adoption Hurdles**: Requires significant education and support
3. **Regulatory Compliance**: Complex cryptocurrency and financial service regulations
4. **Scalability Issues**: Pilot-to-production challenges
5. **Funding Sustainability**: Transition from grant funding to self-sustaining model

## Recommendations for Project Beacon

### Development Approach
- Implement extensive community research phase similar to Austin's MyPass
- Create advisory board including homeless individuals and service providers
- Design for mobile-first, low-bandwidth environments
- Plan for multi-language support from inception

### Technology Strategy
- Use proven Layer 2 solutions (Polygon/Celo) for low fees
- Implement gasless transactions for user experience
- Design for offline-capable operations
- Plan comprehensive security and privacy protection

### Partnership Strategy
- Begin with established organizations (Coalition for the Homeless, Christian Service Center)
- Develop clear value propositions for business partners
- Create simple onboarding processes for partners
- Establish transparent settlement and payment systems

### Business Model
- Plan transition from grant funding to sustainable revenue
- Consider transaction fees from partner settlements
- Explore corporate social responsibility funding opportunities
- Design for expansion to other cities with similar economic model

This competitive analysis reveals that while several innovative projects exist in the blockchain social impact space, Project Beacon has significant opportunities to differentiate through its comprehensive activity-based earning model, local integration focus, and mutual benefit design.
