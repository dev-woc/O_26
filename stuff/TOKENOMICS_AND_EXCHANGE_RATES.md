# Project Beacon - Tokenomics and Exchange Rate Strategy

## Overview

This document outlines the economic model for BeaconCoin (BEACON), focusing on establishing fair exchange rates that properly value human time, effort, and commitment while maintaining purchasing power for essential goods and services.

## Core Economic Principles

### 1. Time-Based Value Foundation
- **Base Unit**: 1 BEACON = $1 USD (stable peg)
- **Rationale**: Simplifies mental math and creates clear value proposition
- **Time Value**: Minimum wage equivalent ($15/hour) = 15 BEACON/hour base rate

### 2. Effort and Skill Multipliers
Different activities earn different rates based on complexity, skill requirements, and community impact:

```
Base Activities (1.0x multiplier):
- Daily check-ins: 1 BEACON
- Basic cleanup: 5 BEACON/hour
- Simple verification tasks: 8 BEACON/hour

Skilled Activities (1.5x multiplier):
- Case management: 12 BEACON/hour
- Translation services: 12 BEACON/hour
- Job training assistance: 12 BEACON/hour

Specialized Activities (2.0x multiplier):
- Mental health support: 15 BEACON/hour
- Legal assistance: 20 BEACON/hour
- Medical support: 20 BEACON/hour

Community Leadership (2.5x multiplier):
- Volunteer coordination: 18 BEACON/hour
- Program development: 25 BEACON/hour
- Training delivery: 20 BEACON/hour
```

## Conclusion

The BeaconCoin exchange rate structure with a 15% service fee balances several critical factors:

1. **Fair Labor Value**: Ensures that time and effort are compensated at living wage rates, with the fee representing a modest 15% increase in earning requirements
2. **Essential Service Access**: Maintains purchasing power for basic needs while creating platform sustainability
3. **Economic Sustainability**: The 15% fee creates a self-sustaining revenue model that scales with platform usage, reducing dependence on external funding
4. **Community Growth**: Incentivizes skill development and community leadership through higher earning rates that offset the service fee
5. **Real-World Relevance**: Stays aligned with actual market prices while building long-term platform viability
6. **Platform Independence**: Service fees enable the platform to become financially self-sufficient, ensuring long-term stability for users

### Key Benefits of the 15% Fee Structure:

**For Users:**
- Predictable, transparent fee structure
- Still able to meet essential needs within reasonable work hours
- Platform sustainability ensures long-term service availability
- Fee scales with usage, so light users pay less

**For Partners:**
- Receive full market value for their services
- No additional costs or complexity
- Guaranteed payment through the platform
- Access to new customer base

**For the Platform:**
- Self-sustaining revenue model
- Scales automatically with growth
- Reduces grant dependency over time
- Enables reinvestment in better services and technology

### The Bottom Line:

A pizza slice costs an extra 7 minutes of skilled volunteer work (0.60 BEACON fee), but this small additional effort ensures the entire system remains sustainable and available for everyone. For individuals experiencing homelessness, the reliability and long-term availability of the platform is worth this modest fee, especially when the earning opportunities provide fair compensation that accounts for the fee structure.

The model recognizes that every coin earned represents real effort and commitment, and every coin spent addresses genuine human needs. The 15% fee ensures this valuable service continues to operate and grow, benefiting the entire community.
